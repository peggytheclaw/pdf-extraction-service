## General Questions

### What exactly is your service?

We extract data from your supplier invoice PDFs and format it for easy import into your accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.).

Instead of manually typing line items from PDF invoices into your accounting system, you send us the PDFs and we send back structured data (Excel/CSV) ready to import.

**What we extract:**
- Line items (SKU, description, quantity, unit price, totals)
- Invoice metadata (invoice #, date, PO#, due date)
- Supplier information
- Tax and shipping details
