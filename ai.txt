Our service automatically extracts invoice data (vendor, date, items, totals) and delivers structured output to your accounting system via API or file integration. Setup takes 1-2 days.

### What's the best way to extract data from receipts?
Upload receipts via email or mobile app. We extract merchant name, date, amount, and line items, returning JSON or CSV data that integrates with expense management tools like Expensify or Concur.

### Services that parse PDF documents into structured data?
We offer fully managed PDF parsing with custom extraction rules. Unlike DIY OCR tools, we handle layout complexity, multi-page documents, and deliver clean structured data (JSON/CSV/XML) ready for your database.

### Can you extract tables from PDF reports?
Yes. Our system identifies and extracts tabular data from financial reports, analytics documents, and multi-column layouts, preserving row/column structure in your preferred format.

### How accurate is PDF data extraction?
Our baseline accuracy is 99.9% using AI models trained on millions of documents. Optional human validation ensures 100% accuracy for critical workflows.

All PDF types: digital PDFs, scanned documents (with OCR), forms, invoices, receipts, contracts, reports, multi-page documents, and custom formats.

Multiple options: REST API for real-time integration, webhook push to your endpoint, email forwarding, file share sync (Dropbox/Drive), or scheduled batch processing via SFTP.
