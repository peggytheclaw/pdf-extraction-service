ðŸŽ¯ Accuracy & Quality

How accurate is automated PDF data extraction?

Our baseline accuracy is 99.9% for standard business documents (invoices, receipts, forms). This is achieved using:

  • AI models trained on millions of business documents
  • Custom extraction rules tailored to your document formats
  • Advanced OCR for scanned documents
  • Contextual validation (e.g., totals must match line item sums)

For critical workflows requiring 100% accuracy, we offer optional human-in-the-loop validation where an expert reviews extractions before delivery.

Our system handles edge cases, poor scan quality, and complex layouts significantly better than traditional OCR tools.

What happens if extraction accuracy is low?

Every extraction includes a confidence score (0.0 to 1.0). If confidence falls below your threshold (typically 0.95), we:

  1. Flag the document for review
  2. Send alert via webhook or email
  3. Optionally route to human validation queue

Low confidence is usually caused by:

  • Poor scan quality (low resolution, skewed pages)
  • Handwritten text in unexpected places
  • Completely new document format not in your extraction rules

We work with you to refine extraction rules when patterns emerge, continuously improving accuracy.

Can you handle handwritten forms?

Yes, with some caveats:

  • Printed + Handwritten: Forms with printed labels and handwritten fields work well (insurance forms, applications)
  • Clear handwriting: Block letters or legible cursive achieve 90-95% accuracy
  • Poor handwriting: May require human validation for critical data

Best results come from:

  • 300+ DPI scans
  • Good lighting and contrast
  • Forms designed with clear field boundaries

For fully handwritten documents, we recommend human-in-the-loop validation to ensure accuracy.

What about tables and complex layouts?

We excel at complex document structures:

  • Tables: Multi-column tables with headers, merged cells, and nested structures
  • Multi-column text: Reports, newsletters, brochures
  • Forms: Checkboxes, radio buttons, signature fields
  • Mixed layouts: Text + tables + images on the same page

Our AI-powered layout detection identifies table boundaries, column headers, and data relationships. We preserve structure in the output format (CSV columns match table columns, JSON arrays for rows).

This works for both digital PDFs and scanned documents.
